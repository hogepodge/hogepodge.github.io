Chris Hoge
Open Source Strategy and Community Development

An active contributor in helping to grow open source user and developer communities for over 10 years. My goal is to grow open source projects and make them more welcome and diverse by:

  • Writing clear and effective documentation that makes projects accessible to new users.
  • Improving the new user experience through better installers, interfaces, and documentation.
  • Supporting self-governance structures that encourage diversity by outlining a clear path to grow from contributor to leader.
  • Expanding the reach of projects through positive collaborations with other open source communities.
  • Creating dynamic spaces for community members to engage with one another.
